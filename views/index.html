Last summer, Chancellor Dirks committed an atrocity. We're here to right his wrongs. BRIDGE THE BROW.

About the Brow

"Bridge the Brow" was founded by Cal students Ana Lua, Gio Zezula, and Dan Gajudo. This non profit organization works tirelessly to restore the facial hair of peace.

After the explosion on the UC Berkeley campus it is more important than ever to bring the brow back for the well being of everyone. You too can help bridge the brow!

Projects in Progress

Currently, the organization is working on an endorsement deal with UC Berkeley's Steel Bridge Team. The team's construction manager has graciously offered to name the back to back national champ's bridge after the cause. In addition, the team will sport T-shirts with the organization's name to inform others of the cause.
